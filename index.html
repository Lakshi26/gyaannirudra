About-Celebrate Life at Gyaannirudra

The essence of life is to celebrate the light (Strength) within us and No celebration can be considered complete if it is not being used as a medium to spread the shine of our light to remove the darkness (Pain) of the community around us.

Therefore, We, at Gyaannirudra, are giving you this medium to sparkle the light within you and share its brightness with the surroundings to help everyone in realizing and sparkling the light within themselves to make your celebration complete.

So, what you need to do is to just pour your feelings, emotions, thoughts, and experiences on how you have overcome your pains or negative moments with the help of your inner light or positive energy and express them in form of stories, poetry, arts, paintings and photographs using the power of your words and imagination so that your creations can inspire the community to sparkle the light within themselves.

Interestingly, this opportunity is open to individuals from all age groups as we believe that No matter what age group you are, there is a light within each one of us which can help others in celebrating the essence of their life.
